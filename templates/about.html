About SentiTube

Understanding sentiment, one comment at a time

Our Mission

SentiTube is dedicated to helping content creators, influencers, and researchers understand the sentiment behind YouTube video comments. Using advanced AI technology, we analyze comments to detect positive, neutral, and negative sentiments, helping you better understand your audience's reactions.

What We Do

Our platform provides instant sentiment analysis for YouTube videos. Simply enter a video ID, and our AI-powered system will analyze the overall sentiment of the comments, giving you valuable insights into how your content is being received.
